Storage, Processing & Retention of Data

  1. Customer data is stored only in secure servers located in India.

    2. Restrictions on use of data
    3. Data destruction protocols
    4. Security breach response and incident handling procedures
  4. We ensure data minimization by limiting collection and storage to only what is necessary for lawful purposes.

Dispute Resolution & Jurisdiction

Any disputes arising from this Policy will be resolved through a two-step Alternate Dispute Resolution ("ADR") mechanism.

  1. Mediation: Parties will attempt to resolve disputes amicably within thirty (30) days of one Party communicating the existence of a dispute to the other Party.
  2. Arbitration: If mediation fails, the dispute will be referred to a sole arbitrator appointed by the Company. Arbitration will be conducted in English, with the seat of Arbitration in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

This Policy is governed by the laws of India. The Courts at Pune shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes arising between the Parties.
