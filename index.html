Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What documents are required for a personal loan?

Typically, you need identity proof (Aadhar, PAN), address proof, and bank statements for the last 6 months.

How long does it take for loan approval?

For pre-approved offers, it can be instant. Standard processing takes 24-48 working hours.

Can I prepay my loan?

Yes, most banks allow prepayment after 6-12 months. Some charges may apply depending on the lender.
