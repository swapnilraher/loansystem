FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from our customers.

What documents are required for a personal loan?
Typically you need: Aadhar card, PAN card, last 3-month salary slips, 6-month bank statement, and address proof. Self-employed individuals need ITR for the last 2 years and business proof.
How long does it take for loan approval and disbursement?
For pre-approved personal loans, disbursement can happen within 24 hours. Standard home loans take 7–14 business days for full processing. Business loans typically take 3–7 days.
Do you charge any fee for loan advisory services?
Our advisory consultation is completely free. We are compensated by our partner banks and NBFCs. There are absolutely no hidden charges from our side.
Can I apply for a loan if my CIBIL score is below 700?
Yes, we work with several lenders who offer products for customers with CIBIL scores between 650–700. We also provide credit improvement guidance to help you qualify for better rates.
How do I track my loan application status?
Once your application is submitted, your dedicated relationship manager will keep you updated at every step via phone, SMS, or WhatsApp. You can also call us anytime at +91 9579005645.
Can I prepay or foreclose my loan early?
Yes, most banks allow prepayment after 6–12 EMIs. Floating rate loans generally have no prepayment penalty. Fixed rate loans may have a 1–4% closure charge depending on the lender.
